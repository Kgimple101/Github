Audience and Goals

Audience

Brainstorming activity to determine audience

To determine the audience of the website and organization, I grouped sticky notes into different categories and looked for connections. I landed on two overarching groups: Need Help and Give Help. These two groups will be looking for different types of information. Within those two groups, I looked for who would be supportive of the organization and who might be skeptical. I placed “families of Young Adults with disabilities” into both “Fans” and “Skeptic” categories since it depended on the family. This activity was greatly beneficial for expanding my concept of who would be considered a member of the audience.

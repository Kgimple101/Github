Design Challenge

What is the problem you are trying to solove?
Design the website to accurately reflect the target audience of young adults with disabilities and their families while maintaining the essential character of the original design.

Take a stab at framing it as a design question:
How can I design this website in a way that will accurately reflect the target audience while maintaining the essential character of the
original design?

Now, state the ultimate impact you are trying to have:
To increase the organization's effectiveness

What are possible solutions to your problem?
To increase the professionalism of the website

Finally, write down some of the context and constraints you are facing:
I currently do not know anyone connected to this organization

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again:
How can I increase the effectiveness of this organization by increasing the professionalism of the website?

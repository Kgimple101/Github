Research

For the Exceptional is an organization that hosts events and opportunities for young adults with disabilities and their families. They do events like proms, cookouts, holiday parties, and more. Their website is reasonably functional and organized, but the style does not reflect their target audience as it appears to be geared towards younger children. In addition, the navigation is often redundent because there are multiple links throughout the pages that go to the same place. The color pallete as well is not clear and the colors clash in an unappealing way. Additionally, the flyers and graphics they create do not connect well with their logo and visual identity.

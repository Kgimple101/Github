Solution

When creating this styletile, I sought to refresh the branding of this organization to make it more inviting and reflective of it's target audience. I limited the color pallete to blue, purple, and grey. I chose these colors because they are fun and speak to the magic of prom. I chose to keep the purple of the orginal color scheme to keep this redesign connected with the original. I also redid the logo with a unique font to reflect the exceptional members of the organization. I chose to go with a simple and polished feel that will be carried throughout the website with clear Calls to Action.

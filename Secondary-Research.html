Secondary Research

Researching the current events surrounding disability in America. .

I looked broadly at disability in South Carolina and the United States. This is a very broad topic, so sometimes the information was very general and some articles focused in on specific disabilities and conditions.
The recent news on disability was varied but not extensive. Articles discuss ] how COVID impacted those with disabilities along with uplifting accessibility stories . What I learned from these articles is that communities need to be intentional about reaching those with disabilities and making an united effort to include them in a holistic way.

It is hard to search broadly when in regards to innovations, since they are so unique to each condition and individual. Because of this, I looked more specifically. I found an article on biotechnology for prosthetics as well as for physiological monitoring and socio-emotional tools for autism from MIT. This section was particularly interesting because it showed the positive impact technology can have on people. I also found an article on adapted toys for children and how they are often inaccessible to families but have such a positive impact on the quality of life for the child. I found two websites that are successful in their design: bestdayfoundation.org www.ecf.net . Their navigation is clear and well organized and their visuals are appealing. The necessary information is easy to find and their target audience is reflected well in the visuals.
